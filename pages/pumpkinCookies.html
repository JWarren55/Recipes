Pumpkin Cookies

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin (canned pumpkin works)
  • Flour
  • butter
  • Brown Suger
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • egg
  • Vanilla axtract
  • pumpkin pie spice
  • sea salt

Recipe

  1. add wet ingredients and mix
  2. add Dry ingredients and mix
  3. shape cookies on baking sheet with parchment paper
    • The dough should be more like a batter
    • If the dough is too soft, chill in the fridge for 15-30 minutes
  4. bake for 350F for 12-15 minutes
  5. Let cool for 5 minutes unless you are trying to burn your yourself
